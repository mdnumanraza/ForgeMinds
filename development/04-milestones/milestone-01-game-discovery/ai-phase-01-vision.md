## What was decided in this phase

- **Knowledge rarity is environmental** (lost/scattered in the kingdom), not personal (the player is not pre-special).
- Phases 1.2–1.5 restructured from "produce separate artifact docs" to "deepening passes on sections of the canonical vision."
- Three companion files in `00-game-discovery/` retired to redirect stubs (except `ai-reference-games-analysis.md`, kept as independent artifact).
