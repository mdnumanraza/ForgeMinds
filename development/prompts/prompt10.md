# FORGEMINDS DESIGN SPIKE

Investigate whether "Beat" should become a first-class content entity.

Goal:

Determine if the canonical source of truth for ForgeMinds should be:

Campaign → Act → Stage → Beat

instead of:

Campaign → Act → Stage → Quest/NPC/Enemy

Evaluate:

* authoring simplicity
* visual editing potential
* campaign readability
* future visual campaign editor support
* validation capabilities
* content reuse
* AI-generated content potential

Deliverable:

content-architecture/ai-beat-centric-architecture-investigation.md

Do not make a final decision.

Provide recommendation and tradeoffs.
